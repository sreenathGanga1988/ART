INVENTORY MODULE

 

INVENTORY ---- MRN

 

 

Here we will see how to make MRN against only Purchase order (PO).

In above screen you will get two block section

1.       Receipt

2.       MRN

 

Lets see Receipt section

Supplier: - Select supplier from dropdown according Supplier DO.

Container: - Fill container no.

In-House-Date: - Fill In-house date according Supplier DO.

BOE: - NA

Remark: - If you want give any extra point so writes in Remark Field.

Finally click on Save Button. You will get MRN Receipt No.

Finish.

 

 

Lets see MRN Section

 

MRN: - For making MRN we will use same screen but fill data in MRN section.

Receipt: - Select your MRN Receipt no from dropdown List. And Press S.

Select PO no from dropdown list and Press S.

Select IDN (Import Document No) from your dropdown list.

You will get PO balance and Doc Qty against PO.

Select checkbox from Item list and put exact Receipt QTY and Extra Qty against the Doc balance Qty and doc extra qty.

Note:-Extra qty will receive only against the extra Doc qty.

if good have extra qty more than extra doc qty please contact to concern merchandiser

 

You will get below screen.

 

 

Finally click on save MRN to Receipt

You will get MRN No.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

INVENTORY ---- GENERAL MRN

 

Here we will see how to make General MRN against only Stock Purchase order (SPO).

In above screen you will get two block section

1.       General Receipt

2.       General MRN

 

Lets see Receipt section

Supplier: - Select supplier from dropdown according Supplier DO.

Container: - Fill container no.

In-House-Date: - Fill In-house date according Supplier DO.

BOE: - NA

Remark: - If you want give any extra point so writes in Remark Field.

Finally click on Save Button. You will get General MRN Receipt No. e.g. SRATRW1100

Finish.

For General MRN Follow the same procedure according MRN Section.

 

NOTE: - After complete MRN Process you will get all item in inventory for sales DO etc.
